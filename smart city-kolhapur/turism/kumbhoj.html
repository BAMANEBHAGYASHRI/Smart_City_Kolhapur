Kumbhoj Bahubali Jain Mandir

                                                            Kumbhoj Bahubali                   

About 27 km to the south of Kolhapur lays this holy place revered by Hindus as well as Jain devotees who pay their respects to the 28 feet tall marble statue of Shree Bahubali and visit the temple of 24 teerthankaras. A temple of Durgamata on the hill also attracts devotees.
